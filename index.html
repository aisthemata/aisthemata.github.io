Aisthemata

is Chicago-based noise musician and graphic artist Daniel Tovar. His debut EP, Critical Forest, is an exploration of the relationship between sound and sculpture using a mixture of processed tones, programmed arpeggios and field recordings. Email for information about commissions and other design work. In addition to making noise and art, Daniel is a Ph.D. student at Northwestern University.
